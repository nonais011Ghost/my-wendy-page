About Us

Dream Flavours is dedicated to crafting delicious, healthy yoghurts using both exotic and indigenous fruits. Our mission is to bring joy to your taste buds while promoting wellness and healthy living.

We started as a small local brand and have grown into a trusted name in flavoured yoghurts, loved by families and communities across Botswana.

As a beginner web developer, this site is also my first multi‑page project using GitHub Pages — combining my passion for technology with our brand story.
